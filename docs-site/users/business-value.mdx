Use this page when you need to explain why cascadeflow exists and why the in-process approach matters to stakeholders beyond the agent builder.

## The Value Story

| Stakeholder | Why cascadeflow matters |
|---|---|
| Engineering | Runtime control where cost, delay, and failure actually happen |
| Finance | Budget-aware execution and measurable cost outcomes |
| Security and compliance | Policy-aware model usage and traceable decisions |
| Operations | More predictable behavior across multi-step workflows |
| Sustainability | Energy becomes a first-class optimization dimension |

## The Core Strategic Advantages

- Inside-the-loop control at model calls, tool calls, and handoffs
- Multi-dimensional optimization instead of single-metric routing
- Business logic injection through KPI and policy intent
- Direct runtime actions instead of passive recommendations
- Decision traces that support auditability and trust
- Structural latency advantage from avoiding proxy-style mediation

## What This Enables

- Agent systems that are economically viable at scale
- Policy-compliant execution in higher-stakes workflows
- Better user experience through lower compounded latency
- Faster tuning cycles because runtime decisions are visible and attributable
